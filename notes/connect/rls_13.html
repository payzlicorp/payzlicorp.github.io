Included in Release

### Enhancements & Updates:

Support Case Numbering:

  • Each support case will now be assigned a unique 6-digit number for easy reference. This number will serve as a searchable identifier, allowing you to quickly retrieve specific support cases.
  • Title Format: The support case number will be prominently displayed in the title of each case in the following format: “Case Number: Title.” This change ensures clear identification and easy tracking of support cases.

Flex Fee Updates:

  • Flex fee 179 has been changed to 180 PCI Comp-Jun Flex fee.

Amex pricing for 796888000439 has been updated.

#### User Management: - Provision to change the type of user from contact to authorized representative/owner and vice versa has been added.

History logs will be updated when Esign is done by an existing Connect user.

#### TSYS boarding has been disabled in the production environment.

Email Notifications and Communication Updates:

  • Users will now receive email notifications when DDA changes are made.
  • Assigned members to a ticket will now be visible in emails.
  • Office 365 email will now connect automatically if not already connected.
  • Closure reason in the ticket cannot be empty; it is now a mandatory field.
  • First names will be included in emails while creating new support tickets.

Support Case Adjustments:

  • Automated Email Notifications: Whenever a new support case is created, an email notification will be sent from support@payzli.com. The email will have a default subject suffixed with the support case subject and will be sent to assigned team members.
  • Communication Tab: The Communication tab has been modified to match the same view as SMS under the parent head case, providing a consistent user experience.
  • Internal Notes: The term “Comment” has been changed to “Internal Notes” or “Internal Comments” for clarity and consistency in the user interface.
  • Profile Pictures: If a merchant does not have a profile picture, it will be displayed in a distinct bright color for easy identification.
  • MMS Handling: MMS functionality has been adjusted to allow MMS only for support numbers. MMS for numbers other than support numbers will not be allowed, and this is managed through bandwidth settings.
  • Internal Comments Access: Internal comments now have the right access control to ensure merchants do not have access to internal comments.
  • Notification Settings: System-generated emails are sent only to individuals who are part of the ticket (members or assigned to). Different types of notifications have been reviewed and resolved to address any duplication issues. Special attention has been given to ensuring notifications align with individual preferences.
  • Organization Name Display: The organization name will now be prominently displayed on the case card and within case details, enhancing visibility and context for support cases. The organization name will be shown in the format: Org Name > Org Name.
  • Attachments in Email Reply: There is ongoing research and development to address issues related to attachments not working in email replies. Our team is actively investigating and will provide an update soon.
  • Permission Adjustments: Comments in merchant profiles have been renamed to “Internal Notes,” and permission to view and modify these notes has been adjusted for merchants.
  • The top-level organization node has been removed from the organization path, and “PayzliUser” has been renamed to “Employee” for a more intuitive feedback system.

Lead and Merchant Account Enhancements:

  • Applications will always be sent to the authorized representative. If the authorized representative is unavailable, the corporate email address will be used in the conversion flow.
  • The statements tab will be visible only if the lead or merchant account has statements.

UI and Design Improvements:

  • The ‘Flex Fee’ edit option now functions properly when clicking on the increment icon.
  • Merchant account owners can not update their risk profiles.
  • UI enhancements have been made to the Forgot Password page and the Sales Partner Comments textbox.
  • Various UI issues in different modules have been resolved, including issues related to Partner Bio Sheet, support communication, profile dropdown, and email UI.

Functionality Fixes:

  • Match data mismatch issues have been fixed, ensuring accurate data representation.
  • Errors related to XML documents while approving merchants have been resolved.
  • Equipment images and Visa POS keys functionality have been fixed.

Filtering Options:

  • Filter options for Sub-organization, User, and Risk Exposure now work correctly in Underwriting and Leads modules.
