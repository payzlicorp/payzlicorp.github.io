Included in Release

This quick release focusses on a large number of fixes and enhancements across Payzli Connect modules such as support, residuals, risk and other modules.

Residuals

  • Residual Program Creation and Residual Reports view has now been added to the residuals module

Support Module fixes

As a part of continuous improvements, we have fixes minor issues and enhancements reported by our QA and larger operations team such as the following:

  • Support emails/notifications, timestamps and other minor issues
  • Improved the navigation by adding a back button across breadcrumbs in the support case details view
  • Showing the latest email instead of complete thread for relevant users
  • URL and duplicate emails and email links related fixes
  • Field related checks and word overflow issues for text copies
  • Hyperlinks are now clickable in SMSes

Risk

  • Fixed minor UI issues and bugs related to the new Risk V1 release

Other enhancements and Fixes:

  • Hotfix to support Visa’s new parameters while onboarding a new merchant. We need this to scale and eliminating an operational step at Visa’s end.
  • Merchant agreement related changes:
  • Created a combined Merchant agreement for Esquire and CBCal to be approved
  • Made changes to the Esquire pdfs as per bank requirements and new fields

Quality Assurance:

The QA team worked on adding Risk and Residuals to sanity scripts post release and UI automation for the profile, partner leads section
