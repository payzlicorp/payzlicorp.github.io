Payzli POS - Release Notes

Version 11.1.0 - November 8, 2024

🛠️ Enhancements

  • PAX Terminal Wake-up Enhancement: The PAX terminal now automatically wakes up when the POS requests a transaction, even if the terminal is in a sleep state. This ensures a seamless user experience and reduces transaction delays.

🐛 Bug Fixes

  • Sale Creation Issue: Fixed an issue where sales were not being recorded in the POS after completing a transaction on the PAX terminal. Transactions are now correctly reflected in the POS, ensuring accurate sales reporting.

This update focuses on improving the integration between Payzli POS and PAX terminals, providing a more reliable and streamlined experience for users. For any questions or further assistance, please refer to our support documentation or contact our support team.
