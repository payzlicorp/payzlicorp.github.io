Appointment Module Enhancements

  • Introduced new appointment statuses and updated booking flows for improved handling of in-person and virtual appointments:
    • New Appointment Statuses:
      • DRAFT: Appointment created without slot blocking, ideal for sending payment links.
      • RESERVED: Slot is temporarily held during booking.
      • CONFIRMED: Appointment confirmed without payment; slots are blocked.
      • BOOKING FEE PAID: Booking fee paid; appointment confirmed.
      • PAID: Full payment received; appointment confirmed.
      • CANCELLED: Appointment canceled; slots released.
    • Enhanced Booking Flows:
      • Merchant Booking: Support for deposits, direct confirmations, and payment links for both in-person and virtual appointments.
      • Customer Booking: Options for upfront payments, deposits, or direct confirmations.
    • Improved Slot Handling:
      • Slots blocked only after confirmation or successful payment.
      • Expired or invalid payment links automatically release unblocked slots.
      • Reserved slots updated every hour based on payment status.
    • Calendar and Visibility Updates:
      • Appointments in DRAFT, RESERVED, and CANCELLED statuses are excluded from calendars and customer views.
    • Automated Status Updates:
      • RESERVED appointments reviewed hourly to transition to appropriate statuses (DRAFT, BOOKING FEE PAID, or PAID).

Technician Service Management

  • New updates for technician-specific service assignments:

    • Booking flow now displays only technicians eligible for the selected service.
    • When adding a new technician, default services, costs, and commissions are assigned automatically, with options for adjustment.
